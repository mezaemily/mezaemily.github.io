Services

I follow a user-centered design process ensuring every step leads to intuitive, beautiful, and effective user experiences

I research the user

I collect data through interviews, surveys, and behavioral analysis. Based on this information, I create visual documents in Figma, such as personas.

Define the problem

I organize insights and define the problem using visual boards in Figma to align the team.

Ideate solutions

I create low-fidelity wireframes in Figma and collaborate in real time to generate quick and effective ideas.

I prototype the solution

I design interfaces in Figma with real interactions using its prototyping tool.

I test with users

I test Figma prototypes with users to identify areas for improvement in the experience.

I iterate and improve

I adjust the design in Figma based on feedback and keep everything updated for development.
